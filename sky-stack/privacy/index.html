Privacy Policy for Sky Stack

Last updated: 2026-06-08

This Privacy Policy explains how Kangsium ("we", "us") handles information when you use Sky Stack (the "App"). We've tried to write this in plain language. If anything is unclear, contact us at jason@kangsium.com.

At a glance

  • No sign-up, no account. You can play without giving us your name, email, or any login.
  • We don't collect personal information ourselves. Your game settings and best scores stay on your device.
  • The App shows ads via Google AdMob, which collects device and advertising identifiers to serve and measure ads. This is the only data collection involved, and it's handled by Google.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Note on "anonymous" identifiers: advertising IDs and similar identifiers are still considered personal data under GDPR even though we never see your name or email. We treat them accordingly.

Why we handle it

We use this data only for the purposes listed in the table above:

  • Operating the App's core features
  • Showing advertisements that help keep the App free

We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share data with

  • Google AdMob (and its ad-serving partners) — for serving and measuring ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Consent for personalized advertising in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland is gathered through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent form shown inside the App.

Your data, your control

  • Clear your local data — reset progress in the App where available, or uninstall the App.
  • Control ad personalization — reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads), and use the in-app consent prompt where applicable.
  • Make a request — email jason@kangsium.com for any access or deletion request relating to data handled on our behalf. We respond within 30 days.

How long we keep data

  • Game settings & progress: until you reset it or uninstall the App
  • Ad-related identifiers: per Google's defaults — see policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Where data is stored

Game settings and progress are stored only on your device. Ad-related data is processed and stored on servers operated by Google. Primary regions: United States and other regions where Google operates.

Notifications & reminders

Sky Stack can send optional local reminders (for example, daily streak nudges). These are scheduled and shown entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to us or any third party. You can turn them off in the App or in your device's notification settings. On Android 13+ the App asks for notification permission; declining it simply disables reminders.

For users in the EU, UK, or Switzerland (GDPR / UK DPA)

Our legal bases: legitimate interest (operating and maintaining the App) and consent (personalized advertising, gathered via the in-app Google UMP prompt). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, data portability, and to object. Because we hold no account or personal profile about you, most requests relate to data processed by Google AdMob; you can also manage that through your device's ad settings. Contact jason@kangsium.com, or lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. International transfers rely on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses as published by our subprocessors.

For California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense, but personalized advertising via AdMob may qualify as "sharing" under the CPRA. Opt out by resetting your Advertising ID in device settings and declining the in-app consent prompt. To exercise these rights, email jason@kangsium.com.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact jason@kangsium.com and we will delete it.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). At rest, ad-related data is encrypted by Google. Your on-device data is protected by your device's own security.

Changes to this policy

Material changes will be announced inside the App. The "Last updated" date will reflect the change.
